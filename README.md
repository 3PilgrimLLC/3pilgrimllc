# 3 Pilgrim LLC Public Research Repository

3 Pilgrim LLC is a for-profit research company focused on the discovery, invention, and reframing of cross-domain primitives and foundational concepts.

## Purpose
This repository serves as our public-facing library: a curated collection of **exploratory research** and unencumbered elements from client projects, released for the benefit of the broader research community and practitioners.

The intent is to contribute reusable building blocks that others may implement, extend, test, critique, or cite.

## Contents
- Major publications (PDF with Zenodo DOIs) – listed in [/library.html](/library.html)
- Core primitives across disciplines – compiled in [primitives.md](primitives.md)
- Minor and developmental notes – in [/notes/](/notes/)
